About Us

We are a team of 5 people, who are trying to index every knowledge source present on the Internet. At present, there is a lot of information available on the internet, but there isn't any platform that is trying to segregate the good content from the bad.

Early participants of the internet thought that the problem of discoverability will be solved with more and more people onboarding, because we have a tendency of highlighting things which are unique, and sometimes important. But what has actually happened is not something that people hoped for. While the internet has increased the overall utility of the society, discoverability still remains an issue (probably a bigger issue now than before), specially when it comes to identifying ways we can educate ourselves!
